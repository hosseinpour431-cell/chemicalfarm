Production Process & Capacity

1-Octadecene is typically produced via two main routes: ethylene oligomerization (dominant LAO route) and wax/FT fractionation & cracking (secondary). The LAO route is preferred for controlled chain-length distribution.
1 Feedstock Preparation
Polymer-grade ethylene is dried and purified; co-catalyst and solvent systems are conditioned for oligomerization.
2 Catalytic Chain Growth
Catalytic oligomerization converts ethylene into a distribution of linear alpha-olefins (C4–C20+). Process conditions and catalyst selection steer cut yields toward C18.
3 Fractionation
The reactor product is separated by distillation into commercial cuts (e.g., C6/C8/C10/C12/C14/C16/C18). 1-Octadecene is recovered as the C18 alpha-olefin cut.
4 Purification & Storage
Final polishing removes traces of dienes/peroxides and improves color/stability. Stored in clean hydrocarbon service tanks under standard combustible-liquid precautions.
